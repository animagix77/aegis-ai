How It Works

A forensic narrative engine that builds evidence in stages and explains every decision.

Step 01

Intake and Case Fingerprint

Prompt, file metadata, usage context, and legal declarations are fused into a single case profile. A deterministic fingerprint anchors downstream results to this exact submission.

Step 02

Cross-Source Similarity Sweep

The engine compares known rightsholders, major film and TV IP, and branded markers with exact and fuzzy matching to catch near-reference language and misspellings.

Step 03

Statutory and Policy Scoring

Copyright checks evaluate rights basis, fair-use posture, CMI integrity, provenance quality, and release channel exposure to produce a transparent compliance posture.

Step 04

Decision Output and Human Oversight

The final output includes findings, confidence trail, and release recommendation. Teams can ask follow-up questions and export a certificate package for sharing.

Compliance Framework

Built for practical decision-making: fast public gut check up front, deeper legal context when needed.

Public Safe Layer

What everyone can use

  • Uploads are screened into low, review, or high concern verdicts.
  • Matches to major studios and franchises are highlighted instantly.
  • Users receive plain-language guidance before publishing.
  • High-risk results are clearly marked for escalation.

Legal Layer

What legal teams can deepen

  • Optional rights, provenance, and fair-use context can be attached per run.
  • Findings are mapped to explicit compliance checks and rationale.
  • Decision packets can be exported with integrity fingerprinting.
  • Attorney review remains the final authority for release decisions.
